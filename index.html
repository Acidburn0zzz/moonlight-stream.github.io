Limelight

An open source implementation of NVIDIA Gamestream

What is Limelight?

Limelight is an open source implementation of NVIDIA's GameStream protocol. We reverse-engineered the protocol used by the NVIDIA Shield and re-implemented the entire thing.

What does this mean for you? Limelight allows you to stream your full collection of Steam games from your GFE compatible PC to any of your supported devices.

Download

Currently, Limelight is supported for streaming to your PC and to Android. Follow the links for information about how to get Limelight up and running yourself.

  • Limelight-android allows streaming to any compatible Android device.
  • Limelight-pc allows streaming to Linux/OSX/Windows.

What's Next?

Limelight for iOS and Windows Phone are in development. Stay tuned!
